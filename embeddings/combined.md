## SERVICES

### Pawn Loans

**Overview:** This service provides fast, secure, and regulated access to cash using gold, jewelry, electronics, or other valuable items as collateral. Customers can choose between pawning for a loan or selling items directly. All evaluations are handled in-store and follow New York State Article 5 pawn regulations.

**What We Accept:** We accept gold, jewelry, diamonds, luxury watches, coins, precious metals, designer items, and consumer electronics including smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras, and game consoles such as PS5, Xbox, and Nintendo devices. All items must be authentic and in working condition unless evaluated for gold or precious metal value.

**How It Works:** Bring your item and a valid ID to the store. A staff member evaluates the item based on condition, authenticity, and market value. You may choose to pawn the item for a cash loan or sell it outright. Pawn loans provide immediate cash and allow you to keep ownership of your item until the loan is repaid or extended. All loans follow New York State regulations including a 4-month loan term with extension options.

**Requirements:** A valid government-issued photo ID is required for all pawn, sell, and buy transactions. Items must be legally owned by the customer. All items must be presented in person for inspection and verification. Loans follow New York State Article 5 pawn regulations, including a 4-month loan term with options to extend.
