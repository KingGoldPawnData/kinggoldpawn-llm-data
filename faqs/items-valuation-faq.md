# Items and Valuation FAQ

## Accepted Items

### What items do you accept?
We accept gold, jewelry, diamonds, luxury watches, coins, precious metals, designer items, and consumer electronics including smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras, and game consoles such as PS5, Xbox, and Nintendo devices. All items must be authentic and in working condition unless evaluated for gold or precious metal value.

### Do you accept electronics?
Yes. We accept consumer electronics including smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras, and game consoles such as PS5, Xbox, and Nintendo devices. All items must be authentic and in working condition.

### Do you accept gold and jewelry?

Yes. We accept designer items as part of our accepted valuables.

## Ownership and Legal Requirements

### What proof of ownership is required?
Items must be legally owned by the customer. A valid government-issued photo ID is required for all pawn, sell, and buy transactions.

### Can I pawn items that belong to someone else?
Items must be legally owned by the customer.

### Do you buy items or just pawn them?
You may choose to pawn the item for a cash loan or sell it outright.

### Must items be presented in person?
Yes. All items must be presented in person for inspection and verification.
