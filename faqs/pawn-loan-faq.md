## Getting a Loan

### What do I need to get a pawn loan?
A valid government-issued photo ID is required for all pawn, sell, and buy transactions. Items must be legally owned by the customer. All items must be presented in person for inspection and verification.

### How is the loan amount determined?
A staff member evaluates the item based on condition, authenticity, and market value.

### How quickly can I get cash?
Pawn loans provide immediate cash and allow you to keep ownership of your item until the loan is repaid or extended.
