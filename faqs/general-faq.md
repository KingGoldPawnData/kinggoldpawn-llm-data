# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is a pawn loan?
A pawn loan is a secured loan where you use a valuable item as collateral to receive immediate cash. You retain ownership of your item and can reclaim it by repaying the loan according to New York State Article 5 pawn regulations.

### What items do you accept?
We accept gold, jewelry, diamonds, luxury watches, coins, precious metals, designer items, and consumer electronics including smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras, and game consoles such as PS5, Xbox, and Nintendo devices. All items must be authentic and in working condition unless evaluated for gold or precious metal value.

### What do I need to bring?
A valid government-issued photo ID is required for all pawn, sell, and buy transactions. Items must be legally owned by the customer. All items must be presented in person for inspection and verification.

### How long is the loan term?
Loans follow New York State Article 5 pawn regulations, including a 4-month loan term with options to extend.

### Can I get my item back?
Yes. Pawn loans provide immediate cash and allow you to keep ownership of your item until the loan is repaid or extended. All loans follow New York State regulations.

## Transaction Questions

### How does the evaluation process work?
A staff member evaluates the item based on condition, authenticity, and market value. You may choose to pawn the item for a cash loan or sell it outright.

### Can I sell my item instead of pawning it?
Yes. You may choose to pawn the item for a cash loan or sell it outright.
