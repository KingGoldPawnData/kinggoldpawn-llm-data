# Accounting, HR, and Daily Operations
## Accounting Cycle
- Daily closeout: reconcile cash drawers, POS receipts, electronic payments; export to accounting suite via secure API.
- Weekly review: compare outstanding loan principal vs vault contents, ensuring coverage plus insurance sufficiency.
- Monthly statements: compile interest income, storage revenue, retail gross margin, and charge-offs for executive review.

## Human Resources
- Pre-hire background checks focusing on integrity and financial responsibility.
- Training modules: compliance fundamentals, XPawn navigation, conflict de-escalation, and SEO keyword awareness for digital communication.
- Performance metrics: loan accuracy rate, customer satisfaction, adherence to 60-second greeting promise.

## Daily Operations Checklist
- Morning: dual unlock, alarm disarm, vault inspection, XPawn sync verification.
- Midday: CCTV spot check, queue time monitoring, Twilio system health review.
- Evening: vault balancing, XPawn ticket reconciliation, alarm arming, and incident log submission.
