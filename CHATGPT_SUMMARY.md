## Services Offered

### Pawn Loans
- Immediate cash loans using valuables as collateral
- 4-month loan term with extension options
- Customer retains ownership until loan is repaid
- Full compliance with NY State Article 5 regulations

### Cash for Gold
- Professional evaluation of gold, jewelry, diamonds
- Assessment of luxury watches, coins, precious metals
- Option to pawn or sell outright
- Competitive market-based offers

### Electronics

### Jewelry and Watches

- Gold, jewelry, diamonds

## Requirements for All Transactions
- Valid government-issued photo ID required
- Items must be legally owned by customer
- All items must be presented in person
- In-store inspection and verification required

## How It Works
1. Bring your item and valid ID to the store
2. Staff evaluates based on condition, authenticity, and market value
3. Choose to pawn (loan) or sell outright
4. Pawn loans provide immediate cash with ownership retained
5. Repay loan or extend according to NY State regulations
